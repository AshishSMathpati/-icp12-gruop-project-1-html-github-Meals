Welcomes to Happy Meal...!

The hotel’s signature meal is a delightful blend of flavor and nutrition, crafted with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Guests can savor tender grilled salmon, perfectly seasoned and served with a vibrant medley of roasted vegetables, quinoa, and a zesty lemon herb dressing. The dish is not only rich in taste but also thoughtfully balanced to support a healthy lifestyle. Each bite delivers a satisfying mix of textures and aromas, offering a gourmet experience that leaves diners feeling energized and nourished. Ideal for both the health-conscious traveler and the culinary enthusiast, this meal is a true highlight of the hotel’s dining experience.
The dish is served on a bed of fluffy quinoa, a protein-packed super grain known for its light texture and nutty undertone. A drizzle of homemade lemon herb dressing ties all the elements together, adding a refreshing tang that brightens the entire plate. This meal is not just visually stunning, with its vibrant colors and artful presentation, but also thoughtfully balanced to support a healthy lifestyle. Whether enjoyed in the elegant dining room or as part of the hotel's in-room service, it’s a satisfying and guilt-free indulgence that leaves guests feeling nourished, energized, and eager for their next bite.
